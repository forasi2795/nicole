United States of America

The country of the red, white and blue with the song of bells and patriotism. This country has 50 states from the east coast with the Atlantic ocean to the west coast with the Pacific ocean. The north American country borders Mexico at its southern border and borders Canada at the northern border. The birth of the United States of America was July 4th, 1776 when the Declaration of Independence was signed. As of 2019, the U.S. is 243 years old. Although the U.S. primarily speak English, there is no official language used at the federal level.
